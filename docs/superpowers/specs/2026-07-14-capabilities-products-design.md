# Capabilities and Products Design

## Goal

Expand Techzad's capability coverage while replacing browser screenshots with a more restrained product presentation.

## Capabilities

Keep the existing two-column capability grid and expand it from four to six entries: Product Engineering, AI Systems, Cloud, DevOps & Infrastructure, Project Management, and Technical Advisory. Cloud copy names AWS, Azure, GCP, and broader multi-cloud work. DevOps & Infrastructure focuses on delivery automation, infrastructure as code, observability, security, and operations. Project Management covers planning, coordination, risk, and stakeholder communication.

## Products

Replace the alternating screenshot-and-copy layout with two equal product cards. Each card uses the genuine app mark in a compact, purpose-built logo area followed by the existing title, description, and links. Cards share one row at widths of 800px and above and stack into two clean rows below 800px. Screenshot assets are no longer referenced by the homepage.

## Assets

Use Sidekick's existing app icon from the adjacent Sidekick project. Use a generated, high-resolution square Orden app icon derived from its existing circular O favicon. Both assets render at the same 88px square dimensions.

## Accessibility and Verification

Logo images use empty alternative text because the adjacent product heading supplies the accessible name. Preserve visible keyboard focus styles. Verify the content contract automatically and inspect the page at desktop and mobile viewport widths.
