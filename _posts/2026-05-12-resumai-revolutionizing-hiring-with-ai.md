## The problem: a saturated talent pool

Recruiters and hiring managers face a common challenge: an overwhelming volume of applications. Sifting through hundreds, or even thousands, of resumes is a monumental task that can take days or weeks, leading to:

- **Manual screening fatigue.** Reading every resume is slow and subjective.
- **Vague criteria.** Difficulty in defining objective criteria for candidate selection.
- **Increased costs.** Longer time-to-hire means higher operational expenses.

## The solution: tremendous efficiency in minutes

ResumAI leverages sophisticated AI to automate the heavy lifting. In just a few minutes, the platform can review thousands of resumes, providing an accurate, data-driven match that ensures you never miss a top candidate again.

## The ResumAI 4-step process

We've simplified the complex hiring workflow into four intuitive steps:

1. **Upload the job description.** Start by uploading your JD. The AI analyzes the text to identify the core requirements and competencies needed for the role.
2. **Automated criteria extraction.** ResumAI extracts essential criteria and assigns initial importance scores to each. This replaces subjective interpretation with machine-learning clarity.
3. **Review and refine.** You remain in the driver's seat. Review the AI-identified criteria and manually update the relative importance of specific skills to align perfectly with your team's needs.
4. **Mass resume upload and matching.** Upload your entire folder of resumes. ResumAI does the rest: identifying the right candidates, ranking them by match score, and even generating tailored interview questions for each individual based on their specific experience.

- **Interview readiness.** Receive a custom set of questions for every top candidate, allowing you to jump straight into meaningful conversations.
